PhonePera PhonePera is the digital financial technology platform developed by Aipaygo Inc. It is a mobile application that provides bills organization, bills payment, and P2P (Peer-to-peer) transactions. PhonePera acts as your personal finance manager for it does not only receive bills and send payments, but all bills coming in and all the payments done are conveniently organized and stored within the application cloud.
No more lines. No more forms. No more wasted time. Completely HASSLE-FREE.
Easy to Use.
Experience true convenience
The PhonePera app offers a user-friendly and intuitive dashboard that makes it easy for everyone to use. Its simple layout is designed to help you have a faster and more efficient way of managing transactions.
Easy to Access.
A personal financial assistant
View all bills in one mobile app as PhonePera collects and organizes bills payables. Users will be reminded every time a new bill comes in and when it is due to be paid.
Easy to Monitor.
Security is everything
With a virtual E-Wallet where users can keep all their information and monitor all money transactions, PhonePera only provides the strongest security there is to offer. Users can send payments, anywhere without worries and hesitations.
FEATURES AND BENEFITS

  • Bills payment, Bills Management, Bills Presentment functionality in one app.
  • Bills sharing option available to let other PhonePera users pay for your bills.
  • Peer-to-peer, on the fly, transactions between PhonePera users.
  • Organize your bill statements and view them all within the app for paperless transactions.
  • Do all your money transactions anytime, anywhere.
  • Store all your bills in the cloud for future reference.
  • Cheapest service fees offered to maximize savings for users.
